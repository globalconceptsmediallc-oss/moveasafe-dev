About Us

Your privacy and security are our priority.

MOVE A SAFE is a family-owned and operated business focused on professional safe delivery, relocation, and installation. We take pride in being prompt, courteous, reliable, and careful with every job.

With decades of combined experience, our team is equipped to handle a wide range of safe-moving situations for both homeowners and businesses.

Our goal is simple: protect your property, protect your investment, and make the process as smooth as possible from pickup to final placement.

What Makes MOVE A SAFE Different

  • Discreet, low-profile service using unmarked trucks
  • Professional delivery and installation for homes and businesses
  • Experience with safes up to 6,500 lbs
  • Careful handling and respect for your property
  • Service built on reliability and customer care

Service Coverage

We proudly serve local customers across the Treasure Coast, Palm Beach County, and Okeechobee, with extended service throughout Florida and into surrounding southeastern states.

Types of Safes We Move

We work with home safes, gun safes, office safes, commercial safes, and other specialty units. Whether your safe is used for firearms, jewelry, important documents, medications, cash, or other valuables, proper handling matters.

Challenging Moves Are Part of the Job

Not every safe move is simple. We regularly plan for tight spaces, stairs, sharp turns, uneven surfaces, and access limitations that require more than just manpower.
